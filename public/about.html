About

Welcome To VistaVilla

Vista Villa is your ultimate destination for discovering luxurious and dream properties that perfectly suit your lifestyle. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for exceptional service, we have been helping individuals and families find their perfect homes for over 10 years.

Our mission

Founded in 2014, Vista Villa was born out of a desire to make the property search process seamless and enjoyable. Our founders, Jon Walter and Brandy Beaver, brought their combined experience in real estate and customer service to create a company that prioritizes client satisfaction above all else.
